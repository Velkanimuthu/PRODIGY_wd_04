Swimming is a full-body workout that enhances flexibility, muscle tone, and mental relaxation. It is ideal for all age groups and fitness levels.

Tennis enhances hand-eye coordination, agility, and stamina. It sharpens your reflexes and mental focus, making it an excellent individual and group sport.
